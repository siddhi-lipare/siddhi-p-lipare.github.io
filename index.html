Curriculum Vitae

I'm an MS by Research scholar at the Centre for Visual Information & Technology (CVIT), IIIT Hyderabad, working on Computer Vision, Video-Language Models, and Forensic Image Analysis.

My research centers on making multimodal models more structurally aware and contextually faithful — from controllable video captioning to detecting subtle image manipulations. I previously earned a B.S. in Data Science & Engineering from IISER Bhopal and joined CVIT as an Applied Research Fellow.
